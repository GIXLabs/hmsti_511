# Overview
One of the best ways to create products that people love is to create a product that solves one of your own problems. In this activity, you'll consider some of the problems you have at your home and brainstorm some solutions you can make using only sheet materials (ideally with a laser).

# Learning Objectives
- Identify problems in the world that can be solved with physical products.
- Brainstorm multiple solutions to the same problem with manufacturing constraints.

# Constraints
- The problem you address must be one you have.
    - Doesn't have to be a big problem.
- Your solutions must be craftable using sheet materials on a laser.

# Deliverables
- Brainstorm 4 problems you have at home that might be solvable with sheet materials cut on a laser. Submit these 4 problems with a 1-2 sentence description of each problem on Canvas.
- Pick 1 of the problems and hand sketch 3 different solutions for that one problem. Submit these on Canvas.
    - If your sketch is not self-explanatory (ask a friend whether they know what the sketch is), please include either more sketches or text to explain.

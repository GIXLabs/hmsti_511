# Overview
You have been tasked with designing a light or lamp based around a lighting puck provided by the teaching team.

# Learning Objectives
By the end of this activity you will be able to do the following:
- Brainstorm ideas to use an existing artifact.
- Read an engineering drawing to get important dimensions.
- Join multiple 3D printed parts together.

# Constraints
- Your design must be made up of multiple parts, joined together through the means mentioned in class.

# Deliverables
- Brainstorm three dramatically different light ideas.
- Sketch each idea.
- Each sketch should include details about where the prints break into multiple parts and how they are joined.
- Submit all sketches on Canvas.

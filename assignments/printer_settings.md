# Overview
There are many slicer settings on 3D printers that can impact your prototyping process, not only the quality of your prints, but also the speed at which you can iterate. Over the last few years, default settings have gotten good enough that you can get some great prints without tweaking settings, but some settings are still important to understand.

# Learning Objectives
- Understand what slicer software is.
- Understand what settings are important in the slicer software.
- Understand which settings can cause your print to fail and which will impact your goals, such as speed for iteration.

# Quiz Format
- Pick a setting you think is important, but not one you think anyone else will pick.
- Read the page on the Bambu wiki (hover over the setting in Bambu Studio, then follow the link that pops up).

1. Which setting did you choose to explore?
1. In your words, what does that setting do?
1. Why do you think it is important?

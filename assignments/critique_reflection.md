# Overview
 Feedback is a critical component in learning and your peers can be an excellent source of that feedback.

 So is seeking out negative feedback, which most peers won't give you without prompting. Negative feedback is a gift and far more helpful for learning than generic positive feedback.

# Learning Objectives
By the end of this activity you will be able to:
- Deliver critical feedback to others on their designs
- Receive critical feedback without defensiveness

# Quiz Format
Answer the following questions in the form based on your mouse documentation:
1. 0.5 points - What are two pieces of positive feedback you received and to whom?
1. 0.5 points - What are two pieces of negative feedback you received and to whom?
1. 0.5 points - What are two pieces of positive feedback you gave and to whom?
1. 0.5 points - What are two pieces of negative feedback you gave and to whom?

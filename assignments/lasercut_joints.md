# Overview
Lasercutter only cut out flat sheets, if you want to make a 3D object, you'll need to join those pieces together. This activity is intended to expose you to a few different methods of joining flat sections, but it is not an exhaustive list (or even close to one).

# Learning Objectives
- Join sheet materials to create 3D objects.

# Quiz Format
1. Please upload a picture of your assembled joints.
1. What were the differences between the joint options?
1. Which joint was easiest to assemble?
1. Which joint do you think is easiest to model?
1. What limitations do the joint techniques have? (for example, have to be at right angles)

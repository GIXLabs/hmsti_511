# Overview
Your team should have several low-fidelity models of the physical form your project will be taking for inspection and critical feedback ("desk crits"). You will probably also have some higher-fidelity models/prototypes for inspection. For the crit, be prepared to show us all of your prototype work in both digital and physical form for us to review (similar to the way we reviewed previous assignments). 

Numerous sketches and design concepts (including sketches in each of your sketchbooks)
Several physical prototypes of various levels of fidelity
Early material, fabrication, electronics, software, and mechanism experiments - as appropriate for your particular project
Be organized and prepared to present your hand-crafted and/or machine-fabricated physical models along with any sketches. Make sure you are able to justify design decisions and identify potential challenges.

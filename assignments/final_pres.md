# Overview
It's time to show your final, high-fidelity prototype of your solution to others and get feedback. You will setup a table with your sketches, early prototypes, and final high-fidelity prototype and present your project to your peers and other members of the GIX community.

# Learning Objectives

# Constraints
- You must present your project during your assigned time so you can receive feedback from your peers.

# Deliverables
- Portfolio quality PDF including the following:
    - Introductory paragraph describing the problem and your solution.
    - Images of your final project in use.
    - Process documentation
        - Images of all your early sketches
        - Images of your handmade prototypes for size and shape with notes about what you learned.
        - Images of your medium-fidelity prototypes to test functionality with notes about what you learned.

# Rubric
| Points | Requirement |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Description of concept |
| 2.5 | 10+ Hand sketches |
| 1 | Size and shape prototype (hand crafted) |
| 1 | Puck dimensions prototype (3D printed) |
| 1 | Joint prototype (3D printed) |

| 1.5 | Execution Quality |

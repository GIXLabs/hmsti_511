# Overview
This is a follow-up to the in-class activity making rapid prototypes with different materials.

# Learning Objectives
After this activity, you will be able to:
- Make rapid prototypes to assess size and shape constraints with simple materials.
- Make rapid prototypes to communicate 3D shapes to peers.

# Quiz Format
1. 1 points - Whom did you work with on your sketch prototype? What's one piece of positive feedback you got on your sketch prototype? What's one piece of negative feedback you got on your sketch prototype?
1. 0.5 points - Whom did you work with on your aluminum foil prototype? What's one piece of positive feedback you got on your aluminum foil prototype? What's one piece of negative feedback you got on your aluminum foil prototype?
1. 0.5 point - Whom did you work with on your polymer clay prototype? What's one piece of positive feedback you got on your polymer clay prototype? What's one piece of negative feedback you got on your polymer clay prototype?

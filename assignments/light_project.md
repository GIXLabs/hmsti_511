# Overview
You have been tasked with designing a light or lamp based around a lighting puck provided by the teaching team.

# Examples

# Learning Objectives
By the end of this project you will be able to do the following:
- Join multiple 3D printed parts together.

# Constraints
- Your design must be made up of multiple parts, joined together through the means mentioned in class.

# Deliverables
- Process PDF (Submit on Canvas)
    - Brief description of your concept (250 words max)
        - Will be used during critique.
    - Sketches - minimum 10, included in sketchbook and scanned into PDF
    - 3+ low fidelity prototypes
        - One must be made by hand to answer the question of what size and shape should your design take (can be cardboard, foam, tape, clay, or anything else you can shape easily and quickly without a computer).
        - One should be 3D printed and test just whether the joint between your parts works. 
        - One should be 3D printed and test the fit with the light puck.
    - 1+ medium fidelity 3D printed prototype (final prototype, and may include lasercut pieces where appropriate)
    - Notes on what you learned in each prototype
    - Submitted on Canvas, must be under 10 mb in size

- In-Class Presentation - Graded in Participation as a separate grade.
    - Bring sketches
    - Bring all prototypes (low-fi and medium-fi)
    - Demonstrate during critiques

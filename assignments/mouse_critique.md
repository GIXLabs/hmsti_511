# Overview
Giving feedback and receiving feedback are critical skills in design and prototyping. Every prototype is an experiment meant to drive you towards a better solution to your users' problems. This activity is intended to give you more practice with these skills.

# Learning Objectives
- Deliver critical feedback to others on their designs
- Receive critical feedback without defensiveness

# Quiz Format
1. What's one piece of positive feedback that you gave and to whom? Please include their name.
1. What's one piece of negative feedback that you gave and to whom? Please include their name.
1. What's one piece of positive feedback that you received?
1. What's one piece of negative feedback that you received?
